Exploring Oldest City – Varanasi

“Where time stands still and the soul awakens”

About the Page

Welcome to a journey through the timeless city of Varanasi (Kashi, Banaras) — a place that breathes spirituality, echoes history, and paints life in every color of devotion, culture, and chaos.

Local Food & Culture

Street food like Kachori, Lassi, Malaiyyo, and the rich tradition of Banarasi sarees, classical music, and art make Varanasi a cultural gem.

At the Heart of Death – Manikarnika Ghat

“Here, death is not an end. It is a sacred crossing.”

In Varanasi, death is not feared, but embraced — as a gateway to moksha (liberation).
The Manikarnika Ghat, where funeral pyres burn endlessly, is one of the most sacred cremation grounds in India. It is believed that those cremated here escape the cycle of rebirth.
